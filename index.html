Welcome to ServiLuck Cleaning

At ServiLuck Cleaning Service, we believe a clean space is a happy space! Whether you’re looking for a quick refresh, a detailed deep clean, or a stress-free move-in/move-out experience, we’ve got you covered.

Our passion is turning messy spaces into spotless havens, giving you more time to focus on the things that matter most.

Our Services

Basic Cleaning

Life gets busy, and maintaining a tidy home or office can be challenging. Our Basic Cleaning service is designed to keep your space looking fresh and organized with minimal effort from you. Whether it’s your weekly touch-up or a quick refresh, we’ll take care of the essentials so you can focus on what matters most.

Deep Cleaning

When was the last time your space got the attention it truly deserves? Our Deep Cleaning service goes beyond the surface, tackling hidden dirt, stubborn stains, and built-up grime. Whether it’s for a special occasion or a fresh start, we’ll leave your home or office spotless and revitalized, top to bottom.

Move-In/Out Cleaning

Moving can be stressful enough without worrying about the cleaning. Our Move-In/Out Cleaning service is tailored to make your transition smooth and hassle-free. From scrubbing every corner to ensuring the space is sparkling, we help you leave a great impression or settle into a fresh, clean environment.
